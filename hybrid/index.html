about axis design

Axis Design is a small architecture practice based in the West Midlands, founded on the belief that good design should be accessible to all. We work across scales—from intimate domestic projects to larger community buildings—always with sustainability and user wellbeing at the heart of our approach.

Our work is grounded in context: understanding the history, ecology, and social fabric of each site before putting pen to paper. We see architecture not as the imposition of form, but as a conversation between people, place, and possibility.1

■
New Build — From single dwellings to multi-storey developments, we guide projects from initial brief through to completion.
●
Conservation — Sensitive interventions in historic buildings, balancing heritage value with contemporary needs.
▲
Consultation — Planning advice, feasibility studies, and design reviews for clients and other practices.
